Ingredients

  • Dark chocolate 180g.
  • Butter 180g.
  • 3 Eggs
  • Flour 75g.
  • Sugar 300g.
  • Salt 1/4 tea spoons.

Preparation

  1. Melt chocolate and butter in one bowl
  2. Take another bowl, break eggs in it, add sugar and salt
  3. Mix them until you get homogeneous mass
  4. Add cooled chocolate mass in eggs
  5. Add flour, mix it well
  6. Pour it into baking dish
  7. Bake in oven at 175 celsius for 30-35 minutes
  8. Done
